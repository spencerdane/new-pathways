About The Practitioner

Hannah Conrad

CBT Practitioner

My name is Hannah Conrad. When I was in my early 20's, I got to witness first-hand the power of CBT. In working with my own CBT therapist, I was amazed how a few simple techniques allowed me to emotionally recover from past trauma. It helped me let go of the emotional baggage I was still carrying. Don't get me wrong; it took real work. The results made such an impact that over the next decade, I continuously looked for ways to apply these principles in my own life and to use them to help others.

In 2023, my husband and I had the chance to move to Bangalore, India. This change afforded me extra time with which I was able to finally finish my CBT coaching course. I was thrilled to get a chance to get more involved with something I'm so passionate about.

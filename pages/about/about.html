About Us

Is a CBT practitioner a therapist?

No. CBT practitioners are not licensed therapists. However, they do have a strong grasp of CBT techniques which allow them to provide practical assistance to those seeking help.

How long should I continue seeing a CBT practitioner?

Every person is different with different needs. However, we recommend somewhere between 8 - 24 weekly sessions. Individuals dedicated to improving their emotional health can put CBT methods into practice after only 1-2 sessions. Many find it helpful to extend sessions in order to form concrete habits with the help of a CBT practitioner.

How much do sessions cost?

Sessions are offered at $80/hr.
